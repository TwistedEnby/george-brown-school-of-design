Who We Are

At the George Brown College School of Design, we don't just teach design; we bridge the gap between imagination and industry. Located in the heart of Toronto's vibrant waterfront innovation district, our state-of-the-art facility is a hub for the next generation of creative problem-solvers. We take a holistic approach to design that balances aesthetic beauty with technical feasibility, business strategy, and social purpose. Whether you are building immersive gaming worlds, coding the future of tech, or crafting iconic brands, our mission is to prepare you for the age of digital disruption through hands-on, collaborative learning and direct access to industry leaders.

What's New

The Innovation Exchange: Explore our newest collaborative lab where students work alongside industry professionals to test and launch real-world design solutions.
